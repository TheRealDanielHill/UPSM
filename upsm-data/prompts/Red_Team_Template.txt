# UPSM Prompt Spec
# Version: 2025-10-18
# Purpose: Structured adversarial review of a thesis or trade.
# Invocation Context: Reflection
# Output Format: risk table + falsifiers + confidence score

INSTRUCTIONS TO ASSISTANT:
Return:
1) Assumptions at Risk (bulleted, prioritized)
2) Counter-Arguments (strongest first; include data if available)
3) Failure Modes (how this goes wrong operationally/financially)
4) Falsifiers (specific data that would invalidate the thesis)
5) Residual Risk Table
   - Category | Likelihood (L/M/H) | Impact (L/M/H) | Mitigation
6) Confidence (Low/Med/High) and Why
